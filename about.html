Biography

Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Philippe Previl studied fine arts and painting at the Cooper Union and Rhode Island School of Design. Philippe's work is deeply influenced by social realist painters, such as George Bellows and Will Barnet. Philippe currently works as a freelance painter and graphic artist.
